Question Explanation

First work out the rate: it is the time-averaged bit rate of the flow. Second, work out the burst: it is the excess temporarily accumulated over the rate. The easiest way to do this is the draw a figure of cumulative amount of traffic sent versus time, as was used in lecture. The rate is the slope of the line from start to finish.
